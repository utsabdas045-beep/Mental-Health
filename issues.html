Common Mental Health Issues

Anxiety Disorders

Anxiety disorders are characterized by excessive fear or worry. They can interfere with daily activities and may include conditions such as generalized anxiety disorder, panic disorder, and social anxiety disorder.

Depression

Depression is a mood disorder that causes persistent feelings of sadness and loss of interest. It can affect how you feel, think, and handle daily activities.

Stress

Stress is a natural response to challenges or demands. While some stress can be motivating, chronic stress can lead to physical and mental health problems.
